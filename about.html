About Us

Welcome to Elmek Academy

Who we area?

Elmek MEP Design Academy is a sister concern of Elmek Solutions, Kondotty. Elmek Solutions has been offering expert MEP solutions and advice since 2009 for numerous clients, builders and architects for a wide variety of projects ranging from residential, commercial, industrial, hospitals, educational institutions and resorts. Thus, Elmek Solutions have established itself as a leading MEP consultancy in the Northern Kerala. Our in-house MEP Experts will train budding engineers by providing Mini-Major Engineering Projects works, where we teach designing of HVAC, Electrical, Fire-Fighting and Plumbing systems for all Types of Building.

What we offer?

Employers look for skills in MEP fields while recruiting. Professional academic qualifications do not make candidates ready to take up MEP jobs. They must undergo industry-oriented training programs to acquire such skills.

Elmek Training Courses (ETCs) are designed considering the current industry requirements. ETCs will contain modules for learning design on-the-job using the latest software applications and practical work experience at construction sites.

The training will be imparted by mentors who have considerable teaching and work experience. Batch sizes will be kept to a minimum to impart personalised training to each batch member.

Career Guidance

  • Members will, on successful completion of ETCs, be issued certificates describing the skills acquired during the course.
  • They will be shortlisted for recommendations for suitable openings with industry organisations that employ MEP professionals.
  • Help and guidance will be given to successful members to get placed, start careers, and perform in their chosen roles.
  • Constant interactions with successful alumni will help newer members seeking career opportunities
